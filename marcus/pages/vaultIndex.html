Vault

About: How to use

This is a tool to help you manage all your passwords while only remembering one.

In order to add an account of your choice, you have two paths:
  1. Add an account and allow us to generate a password for you
    • Select 'Create a new password' on the left menu
    • Select a service you wish to add, enter your username and hit next.
  2. Add an account where you already have a password created
    • Select 'Add existing details' on the left menu
    • Select a service you wish to add, enter your username and hit next.
    • Confirm your details and add your already created password.
    • If at this point you wish to allow us to create your password, leave the password field blank.
    • Hit 'Add'
To view or edit the accounts you have added, select 'Password vault' on the left menu.
  1. If you wish to view or delete your account details, click view and do as you wish.
  2. If you wish to edit your details, press the edit button on the account you wish to edit for, edit your details as desired, and press save.
